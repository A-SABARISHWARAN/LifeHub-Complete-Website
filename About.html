About LifeHub

The Story

LifeHub was born from a simple idea: everyday digital life shouldn't require jumping between dozens of websites and apps for basic tasks. As a solo developer, I found myself constantly needing small utilities - an audio trimmer one day, a unit converter the next - but hated the clutter of multiple bookmarks and apps.

What started as a personal project to consolidate these utilities quickly evolved into LifeHub - a comprehensive portal of essential micro tools designed to make digital life simpler, faster, and more enjoyable.

Today, LifeHub can serve users worldwide, helping them accomplish everyday tasks with elegance and efficiency, all created by one passionate developer with some AI assistance along the way.

Mission & Values

Through LifeHub, I believe technology should work for you, not the other way around. My mission is to remove friction from your digital routine by providing beautifully designed, easy-to-use tools that just work.
